Cover Letter Writing the Inigo Montoya Way:
Four Tips for a Better Cover Letter  Explained  Summed Up

  1. "Hello." - It's best to start off with some type of friendly greeting or salutation.
  2. "My name is Inigo Montoya." - Introduce yourself.
  3. "You killed my father." - Let them know why you are interested in them.
  4. "Prepare to die." - Let them know what you can do for them.
