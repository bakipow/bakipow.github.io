I am the very model of a modern-day librarian

About binomial theorem you'll be teeming with lots o' news
I'll find you cheerful facts about the square of the hypotenuse

I'm very good at finding information that's analogous
To needs most differentiated, important, and fatuous
In short, authoritative information nonsectarian
